Introduction -
In accordance with the directives received from Ministry of Jal Shakti, Department of
Water Resources, River Development and Ganga Rejuvenation, Government of India, New
Delhi vide letter no. T-13014/1/2019-GW Section-MOWR dated 4th August 2020, the re
assessment the Ground Water Resources for the year 2019-20, has to be carried out based on a
methodology recommended by "Ground Water Estimation Methodology, 2015" (GEC-2015)

In order to estimate Ground Water Resources of the State in accordance with GEC-2015

Resolution :
With the view to re- assess ground water resources for the water year-2019-20, the

Terms of reference:- The board terms of reference of the committee would be
as follow:
1) To re-assess annual replenishable Ground Water Resources of the State of
Maharashtra for the year 2019-20 in accordance with GEC-2015 Methodology.
2) To estimate the status of utilization of the annual replenishable ground water
resources.
3) To submit the ground water resource estimates of the state to central level expert
group (CLEG) after the approval of SLC for inclusion in national level report on
assessment of ground water resources.
4) Any other aspect relevant to the terms referred to above.
Time frame- The committee will submit its report on or before 28/02/2021 to the
Government of India after the approval of SLC.
Expenditure- expenditure on account of TA/DA to official members of SLC will be met
from the sources from which they draw their salaries.
